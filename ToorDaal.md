# Toor Daal

## Ingredients

- 1 cup toor daal
- 4.5 cups water (pani)
- 0.25 tsp turmeric (haldi)
- 1 tsp salt (namak)

### Tadka

- 0.33 yellow onion, chopped
- Oil for cooking
- 0.5 serrano pepper, seeds removed (adjust to taste)
- Handful fresh cilantro (dhania), chopped
- 0.25 tsp red chili powder (laal mirch), or to taste
- 2-2.5 tomatoes, chopped
- Black pepper to taste
- Garam masala (optional, very little)

## Instructions

- Wash daal first, then add water
- Add turmeric and salt to pressure cooker
- Turn on cooker between medium to high heat
- When whistle starts, lower to medium and cook for 5 minutes, then turn off

### Tadka

- Cook chopped onion in oil until brown
- Add chopped serrano pepper (adjust amount based on spice preference)
- Cook 1 minute
- Add chopped cilantro and cook 1 minute
- Add red chili powder to taste
- Immediately after red chili powder, add chopped tomatoes
- Let cook down until soft and looks like tadka

### Finishing

- Open cooker, mix daal and keep on low-medium heat
- Add tadka and mix
- Sprinkle black pepper
- Optionally add very little garam masala
