# Red Lentil Soup

## Ingredients

- 1 cup red lentils
- 1 large onion
- 12-15 garlic cloves
- 1 small piece ginger (about half a thumb)
- 1.5 Tbsp Better Than Bouillon chicken
- 0.33 Tbsp cayenne
- 1 Tbsp cumin
- 1 Tbsp turmeric
- 0.5 Tbsp black pepper
- 0.5 stick butter
- Cilantro (measure with your heart)
- 1 large tomato
- Avocado oil
- Salt to taste

## Instructions

- Soak lentils for at least 30 minutes
- Chop all vegetables finely
- Heat oil in pot and start sauteing onion (save about 2 spoons of diced onion to the side)
- Add garlic (save 2 spoons of minced garlic to the side)
- Once the onion and garlic get a little darker than caramel, add all the spices
- Add lentils and toast with the onions
- Add chopped tomato
- Add 6 cups of water
- Close lid and leave on low flame, stir occasionally

### Butter Topping

- Once lentils are soft, grab a separate small pan
- Add butter and a drizzle of avocado oil, start toasting the butter
- Once nice and hot, add the reserved onion
- Fry until crispy
- Add ginger
- Add the reserved garlic
- Once done frying, quickly add the butter toasted onions to the lentils and close the lid
- Remove from heat and wait about 40 minutes to let it cool

### Finishing

- Immersion blend until smooth
- Add freshly chopped cilantro (heavy hand) to soup and mix
